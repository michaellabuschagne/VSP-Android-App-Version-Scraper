24793

Manage your account using your cellphone with the My Vodacom App. Features and benefits include:

Buy bundles:
Buy airtime, voice, video, SMS and data bundles from your cellphone anytime, anywhere. Make sure that you dont go out of bundle using the app to top up on your bundle balances in a simple and easy fashion. Buy a Personalised Double Your Data bundle on the app and received an additional bundle to the same value for FREE.

Balance overview:
Need to check your Vodacom balance? The My Vodacom App offers a balance overview of your airtime, voice, SMS and data consumption, allowing you to view your Vodacom balances from the home screen of your app. Furthermore you can get a detailed balance breakdown by visiting detailed balances for a closer look into your Vodacom balances.

Competitions and promotions:
Make sure to visit the My Vodacom App on a regular basis for exclusive app competitions and promotions. You could stand the chance to win airtime, data and more.

Rewards:
Now you can claim Vodacom and Vodacom partner rewards from your cellphone. From clothing vouchers to entertainment rewards, the My Vodacom App offers a wide array of rewards via the Rewards Every Day hub. Rewards partners include: Spree, Superbalist, Ster-Kinekor Edgars and more.

Account details:
Use the app to access your account details from your cellphone. Log in to check when you are due for an upgrade, update your eBilling information, retrieve your PUK number and much more.

For these and many other benefits in the palm of your hand download the latest version of the My Vodacom App to your cellphone today.
